No Room for Drift

June 9, 2026. Golden hour, Southeast Asia.

The city looks sharper from inside a helmet. The light hits fast, then disappears.

Some days are only clear when they are moving. The heat stays close. The roads stay loud. The mind gets sharper when there is no room for drift.
